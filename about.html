About our Hotels

With a rich heritage spanning over a century, our hotel chain has been a symbol of timeless elegance and exceptional service.

Founded in the early 1900s, we have expanded to include exquisite properties in Berlin, München, and Stuttgart. These hotels have welcomed guests from around the world, offering a perfect blend of classic charm and modern amenities.

Our long-standing tradition of hospitality is reflected in every detail, ensuring an unforgettable experience for each guest.
